Does dental treatment hurt?+
At Elite Dental Studio, we use the latest in pain management and local anaesthesia to ensure every procedure is as comfortable as possible. Most patients are surprised at how painless modern dentistry is. We also offer sedation options for anxious patients.
How long does professional teeth whitening last?+
Our professional in-office whitening results typically last 12 to 24 months depending on diet and oral hygiene habits. We also provide custom take-home whitening trays to maintain your results. Avoiding tea, coffee, and tobacco helps prolong the effect significantly.
What are dental implants made of, and are they safe?+
Dental implants are made from medical-grade titanium, which is 100% biocompatible and has been used in medicine for over 50 years. The implant fuses naturally with your jawbone in a process called osseointegration. They are the gold standard for tooth replacement and are completely safe for long-term use.
How long does an implant procedure take?+
The implant placement itself is a straightforward procedure taking about 1 hour. The healing and osseointegration phase takes 3 to 6 months. Once healed, the final crown is fitted, which takes 1 to 2 appointments. The wait is absolutely worth it for a lifetime solution.
How often should I visit the dentist?+
We recommend a professional check-up and cleaning every 6 months for most patients. If you have ongoing conditions like gum disease or are undergoing orthodontic treatment, more frequent visits may be recommended. Regular visits prevent small problems from becoming expensive ones.
Do you offer payment plans for expensive treatments?+
Yes, we offer flexible payment arrangements for major treatments such as implants, orthodontics, and smile makeovers. We believe that cost should never be a barrier to quality dental care. Ask our team about our easy installment options during your free consultation.
What is the best age to start orthodontic treatment?+
Orthodontic treatment is effective at any age. For children, early assessment at around age 7 helps identify issues while teeth are still developing. For teens and adults, clear aligners offer a discreet and comfortable solution with excellent results. It is never too late to get a straighter smile.
